Intemittent Fasting

What is Intermittent Fasting?

Intermittent fasting is going a certain period of time without consuming any food. It is also known as time restricted eating.

Learn More

History

Discovered in 1945

  • ~ Intermittent fasting was found to extend the lives of mice.
  • ~ The optimum amount of fasting appeared to be fasting 1 day in 3 and this increased the life span of littermate males about 20% and littermate females about 15%
